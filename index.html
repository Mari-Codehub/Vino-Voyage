vino voyage school

school

At Vino Voyage's wine school, guests can learn about the art and science of wine-making from our experienced professionals. Our courses cover topics such as grape varieties, fermentation, and aging, as well as wine tasting and pairing.

Our goal is to provide a fun and interactive experience that will deepen your appreciation and understanding of wine. Come and join us for a unique learning experience that you won't forget!
